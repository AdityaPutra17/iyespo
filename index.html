About IYESPO

IYESPO, the International Youth Empowerment for Sustainable Palm Oil, is a youth-driven initiative committed to reshaping public perceptions and promoting sustainability in the palm oil industry.

Environmental Awareness

Highlighting sustainable practices to mitigate deforestation and habitat loss.

Youth Empowerment

Inspiring the next generation to lead the change in sustainable industries.

Economic Development

Advocating for fair practices that benefit communities and industries alike.

Our Campaigns

We lead innovative initiatives to spread awareness and drive action for sustainable palm oil.

#YESPALMOIL Campaign

A social media movement engaging youth globally to advocate for sustainable palm oil practices.

Workshops and Webinars

Interactive sessions with experts discussing policy, sustainability, and innovations.

Educational Resources

Guidebooks and reports to educate businesses and governments on sustainable practices.
